**Goal:** Meng-upgrade SMC Engine dengan penarikan Fibonacci pada area FVG, hierarki Multi-Timeframe (M15, M30, H1, H4, D1), deteksi wick rejection, dan dynamic Take Profit.

**Architecture:** Modul data loader mengunduh data candle untuk 5 timeframe secara simultan. SMC detector melacak FVG aktif di HTF (H1, H4, D1). Jika harga berada di dalam HTF FVG, sistem memberikan prioritas tinggi pada setup LTF (M15, M30) yang searah. Aturan entry FVG diubah menggunakan level Fibo 0.5 & 0.618 dengan Stop Loss Fibo 1.0 + 20 Pips buffer.

### Task 1: Update SMC Detector with Fibonacci Levels

**Files:**
- Modify: `forex-smc-analyzer/src/smc_detector.py`
- Test: `forex-smc-analyzer/tests/test_fibo_detector.py`

**Step 1: Implement detect_fvg_and_ob with Fibonacci levels**
Modifikasi fungsi deteksi FVG agar menghitung level Fibo 1.0 (SL level), 0.5 (midpoint), 0.618 (golden entry), dan 0.0 (TP level) dari wicks Candle 1 & Candle 3.
- Bullish FVG:
  - Fibo 1.0 (SL) = Low Candle 1 (`Low[i-2]`). SL = Fibo 1.0 - 20 Pips.
  - Fibo 0.0 (TP) = High Candle 3 (`High[i]`).
  - Fibo 0.5 = High[i] - 0.5 * (High[i] - Low[i-2]).
  - Fibo 0.618 = High[i] - 0.618 * (High[i] - Low[i-2]).
- Bearish FVG:
  - Fibo 1.0 (SL) = High Candle 1 (`High[i-2]`). SL = Fibo 1.0 + 20 Pips.
  - Fibo 0.0 (TP) = Low Candle 3 (`Low[i]`).
  - Fibo 0.5 = Low[i] + 0.5 * (High[i-2] - Low[i]).
  - Fibo 0.618 = Low[i] + 0.618 * (High[i-2] - Low[i]).

**Step 2: Add unit tests for Fibonacci calculations**
Tulis unit test untuk memastikan rumus Fibo pada FVG Bullish dan Bearish dihitung 100% benar.

### Task 3: Price Action Rejection Confirmation Logic

**Files:**
- Create: `forex-smc-analyzer/src/rejection_detector.py`
- Test: `forex-smc-analyzer/tests/test_rejection.py`

**Step 1: Implement check_wick_rejection(df, entry_level, direction)**
Deteksi penolakan ekor lilin (wick rejection):
- Bullish (Buy): Candle Low menembus di bawah entry level, tetapi Close ditutup jauh di atas Low. Wick bawah minimal 50% dari total range candle (High - Low).
- Bearish (Sell): Candle High menembus di atas entry level, tetapi Close ditutup jauh di bawah High. Wick atas minimal 50% dari total range candle.
